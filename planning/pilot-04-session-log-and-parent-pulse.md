# pilot instrument 4 — educator session log + parent pulse

The two **data-capture** tools. The session log is filled by the educator straight after each day (≈5 min); the parent pulse runs once at the start and once at the end of the 4-week pilot.

---

## B · parent pulse (start + end of pilot)

Keep it to 4 questions; 1–2 min on a phone. Run **week 0** (baseline) and **week 4** (after) to see movement.

1. **Clarity of value** — "How clearly do you understand *why* your child does art, stem, and language at this age?"
   ☐ not really · ☐ somewhat · ☐ clearly · ☐ very clearly
2. **At home** — "In the last 2 weeks, has your child talked about or shown you something from a session?"
   ☐ not yet · ☐ once or twice · ☐ often   → *(optional) what?* __________
3. **Perceived growth** — "Have you noticed growth in any of these lately?" (tick any)
   ☐ talking / storytelling · ☐ confidence / speaking up · ☐ focus / sitting with a task · ☐ creativity / making · ☐ curiosity / problem-solving · ☐ getting along with others
4. **Open** — "One thing you'd want more of, or anything confusing?" __________

**end-of-pilot only, add:**
5. "Would you recommend this programme to another parent?" ☐ no · ☐ maybe · ☐ yes · ☐ definitely

---

## how the data rolls up (weekly + final)
- **Weekly:** skim the logs → where does engagement dip? which transitions are bumpy? which activities flopped? → make the *one change* per strand for next week.
- **Final go/no-go per strand → trial-to-live:** pacing stable (mostly "about right"), deepened block "held" most days, transitions mostly smooth, experience book fillable in-session, and parent pulse moved up on clarity + perceived growth.
- Whatever survives gets built into the hub (validated session structure, the experience book, the parent positioning) and the strand flips from **trial → live**.
